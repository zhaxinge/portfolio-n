Hi, I'm Xinge Zhang

I am currently a graduate student studying Urban Spatial Analytics at Penn. I am very interested in demographics, cities, transportation and digital products.

Grew up in Qingdao and educated in Beijing and Philadelphia, I have seen births, fast development, transformation, histories, and inequities of cities. I am obsessed with everything about urban development and believed in deep of my heart that data and information empowered the way we understand cities in unimaginable ways.
